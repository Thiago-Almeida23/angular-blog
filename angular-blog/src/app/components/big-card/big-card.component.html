Phoenix Squadron Leader becomes General of the Rebel Alliance

Hera Syndulla, the courageous leader of the Phoenix Squad, finally received the recognition she deserved. After years of leadership and heroism in the fight against the Galactic Empire, Hera was promoted to the rank of general in the Rebel Alliance. Her strategic prowess and unwavering dedication to the cause of freedom inspired her teammates, making her an icon of resistance and hope in a galaxy far, far away.
